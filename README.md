# 🏛️ Artificial Intelligence Robot Laboratory (AIR Lab)
**Kyungpook National University** | Department of Smart Mobility Engineering

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **AIR Lab** at Kyungpook National University (KNU), Daegu, Korea. We focus on cutting-edge research at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Human-Machine Systems.

---

## 🚀 Research Overview
Our research pushes the boundaries of intelligent systems, enabling robots to learn, adapt, and cooperate efficiently in dynamic environments. 

### 💡 Core Research Project
* **Evolutionary Cooperative Robot Development Using Distributed Deep Reinforcement Learning**
  * Developing scalable, distributed deep reinforcement learning frameworks for multi-agent robot cooperation and evolutionary adaptation.

### 🔍 Research Interests
Our areas of interest include, but are not limited to:
* **Intelligent Robots & Autonomous Systems** (Vision-Language-Action Models, Reinforcement Learning)
* **Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) & Human-Machine Interface (HMI)**
* **Healthcare Informatics**
